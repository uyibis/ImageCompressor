Feature

High Compression Ratio

A good image compressor boasts a high compression ratio, significantly reducing the file size of images without sacrificing their quality, allowing for efficient storage and faster transmission

Lossless Compression Option

A valuable feature of a good image compressor is the ability to perform lossless compression, preserving every pixel of the original image while still achieving a considerable reduction in file size, ensuring optimal image integrity for critical applications

Batch Processing Capability

An excellent image compressor offers the convenience of batch processing, allowing users to compress multiple images simultaneously, saving time and effort by streamlining the compression process and enhancing overall productivity
